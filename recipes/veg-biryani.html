Veg Biryani

ingredients

For the rice

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cloves
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste

For the vegetables and masala

  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt/curd
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped mint leaves
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves

Steps

  1. Wash the basmati rice thoroughly and soak it for 20-30 minutes.
  2. Boil water in a large pot and add bay leaves, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. Add the soaked rice and cook until it is about 70-80% done.
  4. Drain the rice and keep it aside.
  5. Heat oil or ghee in a large pan or pot.
  6. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  7. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  8. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  9. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  10. Add yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, biryani masala, and salt.
  11. Mix well and cook until the vegetables are partially cooked and the masala thickens.
  12. Add chopped mint and coriander leaves.
  13. Spread a layer of the cooked rice over the vegetable masala.
  14. Add fried onions, ghee, and saffron milk on top.
  15. Cover the pot tightly with a lid.
  16. Cook on low flame for 15-20 minutes for dum cooking.
  17. Gently mix before serving.
  18. Serve hot with raita or curry.
